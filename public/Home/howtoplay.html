HOW TO PLAY ?

SIMPLY SCORE AS MANY POINTS AS YOU CAN

Eating the small dots all around the maze. 10 points per dot (240 of them). Big points come when you eat 1 of the 4 Big flashing dots called Energizers worth 50 points locate in each corner of the maze.

GHOSTS

In each corner of the room there is a "Power Pellet", which when Pacman eats one, the Ghosts turn blue or yellow. Pacman can get extra points by eating the Ghosts.
